About Us

PledgeWell is India based Fundraising Organisation. It is a free platform, where you can register yourself in moments, and start the drive to help the needy people out there. Our motto, is "Service before Self" and we are wholeheartedly committed to make our society a better place for one and all.

We organise charity events on a large scale, gathering different organisations, universities and schools to be a part of this humane cause. Owing to the COVID - 19 crisis, our country is going through, we have also launched COVID - 19 Relief Fund, to help India beat the Pandemic.
